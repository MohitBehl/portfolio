Learning Via Doing

Development can be quite boring if studied through a conventional learning pattern on the other hand it may be easier to learn it while doing.

Learning via doing means to adopt faith-based learning methodologies, when learn a vast subject like web-development. Make easier projects , encounter many verbos & then aim for major projects by compiling knowledge from simpler projects.

Dividing major projects into modular learning patterns is one of the challenges faced while adopting to this very method, But if done correctly then this project will have a positive impact on your learning. We can often apply these street-smart-hacks in learning most of the things we want.
